FAQ

  • How do I use Snapwize AI?
    Open the app → take a photo → crop to the question area → tap “Solve Problem”.
  • The app fails or returns no response.
    Check your internet connection and try again. If it continues, email us with a screenshot.
  • Camera permission
    If you denied camera access, enable it in Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera → Snapwize AI.
